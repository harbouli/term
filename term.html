DarHome App Privacy Statement

At DarHome, we value and respect your privacy. This privacy statement outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your personal data.

Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number to enhance your experience and provide personalized services within the app. This data is used solely for communication and app functionality.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ robust measures to ensure your information is safe and protected. We use industry-standard encryption protocols and secure storage to safeguard your personal and property-related data.

Third-Party Services

DarHome may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality, but we do not share your personal information with these third parties without your explicit consent.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze user behavior and improve our app's performance and user experience. These tools do not collect personal information.

Consent and Control

By using the DarHome app, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this privacy statement. You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the app or via the contact information provided below.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@darhome.app.
